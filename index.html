How it Started

An old Shopsmith, some wood blanks and a weekend was all Dave needed to turn a wooden bowl. From there, wooden ornaments, bottle stoppers, and more were turned, and projects started to get more complex. Tables were made for daughters and their boyfriends, baseball bats were made special for friends' children, and 'simplicity' flew out the window.
Now, the sky's the limit.
